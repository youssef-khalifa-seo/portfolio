Research Process
01
Keyword Discovery
Used SEMrush, Google Keyword Planner, Keyword Tool, Answer The Public, Ahrefs Extension, and Keyword Surfer to build a comprehensive keyword pool covering every relevant topic, question, and search pattern for each niche.
02
Competitor Gap Analysis
Analyzed 3+ competing domains per project using SEMrush Keyword Gap and Google Keyword Planner — identifying keywords competitors rank for that the target site is missing, as well as untapped terms that no competitor is actively targeting.

🔗 Internal Link Structure Audit & Implementation
Internal link analysis across multiple websites with competitor benchmarking — plus hands-on SEO article writing and topic-cluster implementation on a live training website.
Websites Analyzed
Multiple Websites
Tools
Screaming Frog · Manual Analysis
Implementation
5 SEO-Optimized Articles — Live Training Website
Deliverable
Audit Sheet + Live Articles with Full On-Page Optimization
Audit Process
01
Crawl & Map with Screaming Frog
Crawled each website to map the complete internal link structure — identifying link distribution, crawl depth, and orphaned pages with zero internal links pointing to them.
02
Anchor Text & Link Placement Analysis
Reviewed all anchor text for descriptiveness, keyword alignment, and placement context — distinguishing between contextual links within content and navigational links in menus and footers.
03
Competitor Benchmarking
Analyzed 3+ competitor websites per project to understand their linking patterns — identifying structural advantages to replicate and mistakes to avoid in the recommendations.
04
Recommendations & Next Steps
Delivered structured audit sheets with issue classification and a clear next-steps plan — prioritizing links between topically related pages within the same market segment.
